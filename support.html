Before You Contact Us

For faster help, please include:

  • Your player name or player ID, if available
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The Treasure Master app version
  • A short description of what happened and any steps that reproduce it
  • A screenshot, if it helps explain the issue

Please never send passwords, full payment card details, or other sensitive information.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure Treasure Master and iOS are updated to the latest available versions.
  • Close and reopen the app.
  • Check your internet connection, then restart your device.
  • Do not delete the app unless your progress is linked or backed up.

In-App Purchases

If a purchase completed but the item did not appear, restart the app and use any available restore-purchases option. If it is still missing, email us with the purchase date, item, and Apple order ID.

Billing and refund requests are handled by Apple through Report a Problem.
