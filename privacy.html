Privacy Policy

Treasure Master: Idle RPG

Developer: Frontier Signal Games

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Frontier Signal Games operates Treasure Master: Idle RPG. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and the choices available to players.

Information We Collect

Treasure Master: Idle RPG may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Game progress, inventory, currency balances, upgrades, battle progress, cards, pets, clans, rankings, and other gameplay data.
  • Player profile information such as display name, avatar, clan information, public profile stats, chat messages, reports, and blocks.
  • Device and app information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, diagnostics, and performance data.
  • Advertising identifiers and consent choices when ads are shown through Google AdMob.
  • Purchase information processed by Google Play or Apple App Store for in-app purchases.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Save and sync game progress.
  • Provide gameplay features such as leaderboards, clans, public profiles, chat, battles, rewards, and purchases.
  • Show rewarded ads and manage ad consent where required.
  • Detect bugs, crashes, abuse, cheating, or misuse of the game.
  • Respond to support, report, block, moderation, and account deletion requests.

Third-Party Services

The game may use third-party services that collect information according to their own privacy policies, including:

  • Google Firebase for backend services, database storage, authentication, analytics, diagnostics, or crash reporting.
  • Google AdMob for rewarded ads and advertising consent management.
  • Google Play Billing and Apple In-App Purchase for payment processing.

Ads and Consent

Treasure Master: Idle RPG may show rewarded ads. In regions where consent is required, the game may ask for permission before personalized ads are shown. Players may be able to change privacy or ad choices through the app settings or device settings.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not receive or store full payment card details. Store platforms may provide purchase confirmations needed to unlock purchased content.

Public and Social Features

Some information may be visible to other players, including display name, avatar, clan, leaderboard position, public profile stats, equipped items, cards, and chat messages. Players should not share personal information in names, clan text, or chat.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers needed to operate the game, comply with law, prevent fraud or abuse, process purchases, deliver ads, or provide support.

Data Retention and Deletion

Gameplay and account-related data may be kept while your account is active or as needed to operate the game, provide support, prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

To request account or data deletion, contact us at frontiersignalgames@gmail.com.

Children

Treasure Master: Idle RPG is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect player information, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact
